    <h1>Bread & Strawberry Jam</h1>

    <p>A simple homemade strawberry jam recipe served with toasted bread.</p>

    <h2>Ingredients</h2>
    <ol>
        <li>2 slices of bread</li>
        <li>1 cup chopped strawberries</li>
        <li>1/4 cup sugar</li>
        <li>1 tbsp lemon juice</li>
        <li>Butter (optional)</li>
    </ol>

    <h2>Instructions</h2>

    <p class="step">Step 1: Cook strawberries, sugar, and lemon juice over medium heat until thick.</p>
    <p class="step">Step 2: Toast the bread slices until golden brown. Spread butter if desired.</p>
    <p class="step">Step 3: Spread jam on toast. Serve and enjoy!</p>
